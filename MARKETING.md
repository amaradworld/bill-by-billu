### Post 2: GST Awareness (Educational)

5 GST mistakes that cost Indian businesses lakhs every year:

1️⃣ **Wrong HSN codes** — Using wrong HSN codes can lead to penalty. Bill By Billu auto-detects HSN codes based on product descriptions.

2️⃣ **Missing place of supply** — Forgetting to mention place of supply = wrong CGST/SGST vs IGST calculation. We handle this automatically.

3️⃣ **Manual GSTR-1 compilation** — Spending hours copying data to Excel for GST filing. Our GSTR-1 export takes 2 seconds.

4️⃣ **No input tax credit tracking** — Not claiming ITC on purchases = paying more tax than needed. Our expense tracker categorizes everything with ITC.

5️⃣ **Mixing personal and business expenses** — This is a red flag for GST officers. Keep them separate with our expense module.

I built Bill By Billu to solve all of these problems. It's free to try — 5 invoices/month, no credit card needed.
