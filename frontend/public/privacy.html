Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026 | Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Bill By Billu ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Bill By Billu invoicing and GST application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, business name, phone number, GSTIN, and business address.

Invoice Data: We process invoice details, customer information, product/service details, pricing, and payment status that you enter into the app.

Customer Data: Customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, and GSTIN that you add for invoicing purposes.

Expense Data: Expense details, categories, amounts, and dates that you log for bookkeeping.

Payment Information: Razorpay API keys (stored encrypted) for generating payment links. We do not store credit card or bank details.

Usage Data: Log data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and interaction patterns.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, and camera permissions (for receipt scanning features).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To generate GST-compliant invoices and GSTR-1 reports
  • To send invoice PDFs and payment links via WhatsApp (with your explicit action)
  • To process payments through Razorpay
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, account notifications)
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with GST and tax regulations
  • To communicate service updates (with opt-out)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Razorpay: For payment link generation (subject to their privacy policy)
  • WhatsApp Business API: For sending invoices (only when you explicitly choose to send)
  • Google: For Google Sign-In authentication (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Infrastructure Providers: Vercel, Render, Supabase — for hosting and database
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or government regulation

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
  • JWT-based authentication with secure token storage
  • Google OAuth 2.0 for secure sign-in
  • RBAC (Role-Based Access Control) for team accounts
  • Password hashing with industry-standard algorithms
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days. Invoice and GST records may be retained for 6 years as required by Indian tax law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Export: Export invoices and data in CSV/JSON format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from promotional communications

7. WhatsApp Integration

When you choose to send an invoice via WhatsApp, we share the invoice details (customer name, amount, invoice number) with the WhatsApp Business API. We do not store your WhatsApp conversations. This feature requires your explicit action each time.

8. Google Sign-In

If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or other data. You can revoke access anytime via your Google account settings.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on servers in India. By using the Service, you consent to data processing in India.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification.
